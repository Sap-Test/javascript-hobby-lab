Introduction

This document has examples of many different types of HTML5 elements. Each tag is interpreted differently by the browser. Some of these tags may not look visually different, but can provide assistance to accessibility software or search engines to find relevant content.

Instructions

  1. Reading is a good hobby that everyone can do. All you need to read is a book that you actually want to read. You can read your book almost everywhere.
  2. Replace the above introduction with a brief introduction to this hobby of yours.
  3. In the style sheet, change the background-color property of the header and footer to a different color.
  4. Replace the image in aside to the right with an image representing your hobby.
  5. Add a class to the "Did you know?" header. In styles.css, add a rule to italicize the header.
  6. Replace the "Did you know?" content with an interesting fact about your hobby.
  7. Replace these instructions with a list of three "Resources" to find more information about your hobby. These should link to external sites.
  8. Show your instructor when you are finished.
